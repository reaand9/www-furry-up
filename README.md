Furry up: Rescue Animal Adoption Platform

This project is an online animal adoption platform designed to connect rescue shelters with compassionate pet lovers. The platform showcases rescued and stray animals that are available for adoption, making it easier for users to find a pet that matches their lifestyle and give them a loving forever home.

Users can browse available pets, view detailed profiles containing important information such as the pet's name, age, personality, and photos, and learn more about each animal before proceeding with the adoption process. The platform helps shelters present their animals in an organized and accessible way while encouraging responsible pet adoption.

Features:
Browse rescued and stray animals available for adoption
View detailed pet profiles with photos and descriptions
Display shelter information and pet details
User-friendly interface for exploring available pets
Promote responsible pet adoption and animal welfare
Purpose

The goal of this platform is to increase the visibility of rescued animals, simplify the adoption process, and help more shelter pets find safe, permanent, and loving homes.
